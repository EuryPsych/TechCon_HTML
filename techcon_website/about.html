Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 by a group of technology enthusiasts who wanted to create a platform for sharing cutting-edge innovations. What started as a small gathering of 200 attendees has now grown into one of the premier tech conferences globally, with over 10,000 participants annually.

Over the years, TechCon has been hosted in various tech hubs around the world, including San Francisco, Berlin, Tokyo, and now returning to our original home in Silicon Valley for our 15th anniversary edition.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to bridge the gap between emerging technologies and practical implementation. We believe in fostering innovation through collaboration and knowledge sharing.

Our core principles:

  • Democratizing access to technological knowledge
  • Creating networking opportunities across disciplines
  • Showcasing groundbreaking research and applications
  • Promoting diversity and inclusion in tech
